Advice I Wish I Would Have Got

  • Continue to read even if you don't understand something.
  • Look for newbie resources, when regular docs are confusing you more than helping you.
  • listen to podcasts on your commute.
  • keep a list of all the books your classmates recommend.
  • build yourself tools, websites, apps... a.k.a practice
  • give yourself permission to blaze your own trail.
