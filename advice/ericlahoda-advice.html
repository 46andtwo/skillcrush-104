Hi , I'm Eric and I have some advice for you!

coding is like practicing a musical intrument

When you watch someone play an instrument, you may think, "I can do that if I knew a bit of music and finger positions". However, everyone needs to practice. The same is true with coding.

Mistakes are your friends

It can be frusrating to not get the answers correct. But each attempt that you fail you learn what can and cannot be done.

Plus those mistakes force you to practice again and again.
